Product Concept · 2025

Gather

The social planning app that lives
where your friends already are.

Core Insight
"The chat is the artifact.
The plan is a living summary of it."
The Problem
Great plans die in scroll-back.

Friends are fragmented across WhatsApp, Messenger, and Notes. Planning ideas get buried. One person carries all the load. People flake because commitment feels vague.

💬 Plans get buried

Ideas disappear in fast-moving group chats

😮‍💨 One person does it all

Coordination falls on whoever cares most

📌 Saves go nowhere

Instagram pins & Maps lists never connect to the right people

🤷 Vague commitment

"Yeah sounds good" ≠ showing up

Doodle treats the form as the product.
Gather treats the conversation as the product.
Every tool today pulls you out of the chat to plan.
Gather keeps the plan inside the conversation.
The Solution
Three pillars, one flywheel.

Each pillar reinforces the others — discovery fuels planning, planning fuels memory, memory fuels discovery.

💬

Group Chat + Planning

Plans live inside the conversation. A live Glance bar, instant RSVPs, and a plan drawer — all without leaving the chat.

⭐

Personal Companion

Your private social wishlist. Saves from Instagram, Maps, and Notes — context-aware suggestions dropped into the right chat at the right moment.

🔭

Social Discovery

A curated magazine, not an algorithmic feed. One action per item: Save. Individual intent meets group action.

Interaction Design
Four modes, one seamless flow.

The depth of engagement matches the moment — from a glance to a full planning session, without ever leaving the conversation.

Glance
Always-visible bar at the top of chat. "Fri night — in progress · 3 in, 2 pending."
Snap
Tap the bar. Summary view: date, vibe, who's in, one decision needed.
Full Board
Plan drawer slides up. Full RSVP, details, lock-in. Never leaves the conversation.
Companion
Private wishlist. Context-aware. Surfaces when the moment is right.

The App
Six screens, one experience.

A living mockup is already built and interactive. All screens share one design system and one data model.

🏠 Home Social living room. This week / month / someday.
💬 Chats Groups with colour-coded plan indicators.
📋 Chat + Plan Glance bar, plan card, compose with 📅 button.
⭐ Companion Wishlist with smart suggestions per group.
🔭 Explore Curated editorial. One action: Save.
📖 Memory Group scrapbook, stats, vibes, milestones.
Competitive Position
Not competing with Doodle.

We're competing with WhatsApp. The real bar is being at least as good as what people already use — and meaningfully better for plans.

WhatsApp
Plans get buried in chat noise. No structure, no commitment.
Doodle
Pulls you out of the chat. A form, not a friend.
Partiful
Event pages only. No full lifecycle, no group dynamic.
Google Cal
Productivity-first, not social-first. Feels like work.
